## Headline
Wodapalooza Expands to Mexico City — Three-Day Event Set for December 2026

## Article Body (under 300 words — self-contained)

Wodapalooza is heading south of the border. The premier CrossFit competition series announced it will expand to Mexico City with a three-day event scheduled for December 4–6, 2026, marking the brand's third location alongside Miami Beach and Southern California.

The announcement came via a promo video posted on Wodapalooza's official social media channels on the eve of the 2026 Miami Beach event. The video also confirmed Wodapalooza SoCal for September 25–27, 2026. Details on divisions, venue, and registration for Mexico City remain limited, but more information is expected soon.

The expansion is significant for a brand that has operated almost exclusively on U.S. soil since its founding in 2012. Wodapalooza has grown from a single Miami competition into one of the most recognized community events in functional fitness, attracting thousands of athletes and spectators each year. The 2024 launch of WZA SoCal with the TYR Cup proved the model could scale beyond South Florida, and Mexico City represents the next step — taking the brand international.

Wodapalooza already has ties to the Latin American fitness community through its LATAM Cup series, which features partner events across Colombia, Mexico, Ecuador, Guatemala, Venezuela, and Puerto Rico. The Black Challenge, one of those partner events, is already held in Mexico City. Hosting a full Wodapalooza event there signals confidence in the region's growing CrossFit market.

The December timing gives athletes a late-season competition option. With the Games wrapping up in July and Semifinals running through spring, a December event fills a gap in the competitive calendar and gives the community a major destination event to close out the year. Registration details and division breakdowns are expected later this year.
