Is Hopper’s Groin a Problem for Mayhem?

Jayson Hopper mentioned a nagging groin issue on Boys Interrupted after Quarterfinals and then again in a treadmill IG story, saying he’s “slowly turning the corner.” With the Mayhem Classic just a week away and Games spots on the line, fans are wondering how much this will affect his performance?

Sources: Boys Interrupted podcast; @jhopper3 Instagram story

Engagement News

Grace Walton & Zane Healey Are Engaged

Grace Walton shared the news on Instagram that she and Zane Healey are engaged, giving CrossFit fans one of the happiest relationship updates to hit the feed in a while.

The post quickly caught attention as athletes and fans jumped into the comments to celebrate the couple, with the first photo delivering a full on engagement announcement moment.

For Walton, who has become one of the rising names in the sport, the update gave followers a more personal glimpse away from the competition floor and into a major life milestone.

It is not drama this time, just good news, and the CrossFit world seems more than happy to rally around it.

Scheduling Gossip

Do As I Say, Not As I Do?

Twelve days ago, The Barbell Spin’s new Season Report with Tyler Watkins signed off with a confident “see you next week,” but the follow up episode still hasn’t appeared.

This comes on the heels of Watkins leaving the Kill Taylor show and group text thread, after giving unsolicited feedback about changes he thought needed to be made to the show. One was them needing more consistent times and dates, criticism that he doubled down on during a live stream with Andrew Hiller.

Yet his own new series has now missed its promised weekly return, and the regular Wednesday Spin show has twice been shifted to Tuesday, raising questions about where that same standard of consistency is when the roles are reversed.

For now, it feels like another classic case of “do as I say, not as I do” and the CrossFit audience is starting to notice.
