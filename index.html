Hiller Leaves WZA After Access Dispute — “I’m Packing Up My Shit”

According to Sevan Matossian on CF Network, Andrew Hiller was allegedly paid by Wodapalooza (Dylan) to cover the event with full floor access — hotel, airfare, and glasses included. Hiller had one stipulation: complete floor access with no restrictions. When he went backstage during the CrossFit Open 26.3 announcement portion, he was told his access only covered Wodapalooza, not the CrossFit-run event. After a back-and-forth, Hiller allegedly filmed the exchange with his smart glasses, allegedly said “fine, no problem,” and left Miami.

CEO Search

CrossFit CEO Search Underway After Faul Exit

Don Faul resigned as CrossFit CEO with approximately 3 days notice during the Open. An executive search firm has been engaged. Coffee Pods & Wods debated replacements — Taylor Self dismissed Bruce Edwards (Planet Fitness CEO) as “Eric Roza 2.0” and pushed for a Dana White type. All three agreed the next CEO needs recent gym ownership experience.

Making Amends

Hiller Apologizes to Taylor Self — Live on CF Network

Andrew Hiller joined Sevan and Taylor Self live on the CF Network YouTube channel. Hiller opened by apologizing to Taylor, saying it “rips at his brain” having friends in the space while also wanting to make content. Dave Castro later dismissed Hiller as “not essential to CrossFit.”
