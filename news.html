Mayhem Classic Will Be Livestreamed Free — Froning & McKernan Confirm Full Broadcast

Rich Froning and Rory McKernan confirmed on today’s episode of The Rich Froning Podcast that the 2026 Mayhem Classic will be fully livestreamed and free to watch. The first Semifinal of the 2026 CrossFit season runs April 17–19 at CrossFit Mayhem in Cookeville, Tennessee.

Froning acknowledged the financial hit from last year’s broadcast: “Last year we lost a lot of money. So this year we’re going to try to lose a little less money.” The production is described as “extremely expensive” and is coming directly out of Froning’s pockets. To help offset costs, Mayhem is introducing an optional online community event where participants can do modified versions of the competition workouts alongside the elite athletes for a nominal fee.

The online participation will use a golf-style flights system with RX and scaled divisions, with workouts designed to be done at any affiliate without specialized equipment. The broadcast itself will not be behind a paywall — Froning and the team explicitly rejected that model. Single-day spectator passes are now available for in-person attendance. The production team is still being finalized, with Flow Elite among those in discussions.

Source: The Rich Froning Podcast Ep. 075 — CrossFit Mayhem YouTube

Wodapalooza Starts Tomorrow — Full Elite Schedule Released

Gymreapers Wodapalooza Miami Beach kicks off Thursday, March 12 and runs through Sunday, March 15. The elite individual division competes Thursday and Friday across five events including 26.3. The Barbell Spin published the full event schedule: Thursday features 26.3 plus “No Easy Way Down” (6:31 PM women, 7:28 PM men). Friday brings “Complex, Actually,” “Heavy After Dark,” and “Press & Impress.” Elite teams of three take over Saturday and Sunday with six events.

Source: The Barbell Spin

March 11, 2026

26.3 Tomorrow — Brandon vs. Loewen vs. Kerstetter Live from Miami

The final Open workout drops Thursday at 12 PM PT / 3 PM ET from Wodapalooza. Danielle Brandon, Arielle Loewen, and Olivia Kerstetter will go head-to-head in the 26.3 showdown. Scores are due by Monday, March 16 at 5 PM PT. Community predictions lean toward barbell movements — multiple commentators expect ascending clean & jerks since the first two workouts had no loaded barbell work.

Source: CrossFit.com

March 11, 2026

2026 Games Tickets on Sale Next Week — Prices Start at $360

CrossFit released full ticketing details for the 2026 Games at the SAP Center in San Jose (July 24–26). Only three-day Arena Ticket Packages are available. Affiliate presale March 17, Open participants March 19, general public March 24. Prices range from $360 to $800.

Source: CrossFit Games
